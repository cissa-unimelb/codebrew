Eligibility

  • Each individual participating in Codebrew 2021 must purchase a ticket via EVENTBRITE-LINK.
  • You are welcome to organise a team before the event, but if you don't have a team, don't worry! You can purchase the ticket as an individual and form/join teams on our DISCORD-CHANNEL-LINK. Once you have finalised your team, fill out TEAM-FORM-LINK. Only one person per team needs to fill this out.
  • Teams must comprise of 4-5 people. Teams with less than the required number will be asked to merge. Codebrew organisers reserve the right to relax this restriction at our discretion.
  • All current university students are welcome to compete!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Codebrew?

Codebrew is an annual hackathon run by students, for students. Hack together a prototype based on a theme over the course of 27 hours.

Who can attend?

Any university student can attend Codebrew!

When is it?

Codebrew will be held from the 16th (Friday) to the 19th (Monday) of April.

What is the theme?

This year's theme will be on preparing for the post-COVID era!

What is the team size?

4-5 people per team.

How much is a ticket?

$15 per head.

Why should I go?

Meet like-minded students, network with industry leaders, give yourself an edge when applying for internships and grad jobs, win prizes and make a difference!

Who runs Codebrew?

Codebrew is run by CISSA, the premier computing club at the University of Melbourne.
