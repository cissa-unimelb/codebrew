How to Sign Up?

  1. 1. Purchase a ticket from Eventbrite.
  2. 2. Join our Discord server.
  3. 3. If you don't have a team, don't worry! You can form or join teams in the #team-finder channel on Discord.
  4. 4. Once you have a team & individually purchased tickets, fill out the team registration form (one per team).
  5. 5. You're all set for Codebrew 2023!

Eligibility

  • All current university students are welcome to compete!
  • Teams must comprise of 3-5 people. Teams with less than the required number will be asked to merge. Codebrew organisers reserve the right to relax this restriction at our discretion.
  • There will also be a team making session at the start of Codebrew where we will form a team for you!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Codebrew?

Codebrew is an annual hackathon run by students, for students. Hack together a prototype based on a theme over the course of 72 hours.

Who can attend?

Any university student can attend Codebrew!

When is it?

Codebrew will be held from the 13th (Thursday) to the 16th (Sunday) of April.

Where is it?

Codebrew will be in person.

What is the theme?

This year's theme will be "Turning the Tide!"

What is the team size?

3-5 people per team.

How much is a ticket?

$10 for early bird tickets (ending 3rd April), $20 for general tickets.

Why should I go?

Meet like-minded students, network with industry leaders, give yourself an edge when applying for internships and grad jobs, win prizes and make a difference!

Who runs Codebrew?

Codebrew is run by CISSA, the premier computing club at the University of Melbourne.

Is there free food?

Yes! There will be free food in person
